Our Story

Who We Are

Born from a Passion for Beauty

Wanjiku's Beauty Hub was born in the heart of Nairobi from one woman's belief that every person deserves to feel extraordinary. What started as a small home studio in 2018 has grown into one of Nairobi's most-loved full-service beauty salons.

Our founder, Wanjiku, trained in Nairobi and Johannesburg before bringing world-class techniques back home. She built a team of passionate professionals who share her dedication to craft, care, and community.

We're more than a salon — we're a space where you come as you are and leave as the best version of yourself.
